## Patchnotes 4.2.28

### New features
- Agenda: Meeting admins can copy agenda items to another meeting that they administer. Optionally, the list of speakers with completed and open speeches, moderation notes, and attachments can be copied
- Motions > Export: Attachments to motions can be included in the export of motions.

### Optimizations
- Dashboard: Long meeting titles are abbreviated on the dashboard.
- Organization admins: Organization admins cannot set themselves to inactive.
- Deletion concept for participants: Users who were registered as supporters and have been deleted are listed as “Deleted users.”
- Various UI improvement

### Bug fixes
- Agenda > Poll: All vote weights are displayed correctly when exporting polls.
- Motions > Voting: Simultaneously ending, publishing, and anonymizing roll-call votes is working again.
- Participants > Mandate check: The view is also readable in dark mode.
- Settings > Default voting groups: The presets for voters are displayed and applied correctly.
